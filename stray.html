Stray Gameplay Sound Design and Alternative Scoring

This project was completed on April 3rd, 2023

Overview

This assignment was completed for my ATLS 3200 course, Sound Design. I was assigned to create a sound design for a videogame. I chose the videogame "Stray" because I was drawn to the cat character and the robot designs. I was responsible for creating a brand new unique scoring for the videogame within Ableton Live. I was able to sample some sounds, which I did, such as the cat sounds and the guitar. Otherwise, most of the rest of the sounds were self created, and edited within Ableton Live to fit the atmosphere of the game.
