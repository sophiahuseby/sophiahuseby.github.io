Packaging Design

Overview

For this assignment, I was tasked to create a brand new brand and packaging design for a made up brand. I needed to create the branding materials, packaging design, and final mockup for this imagined product.

Inspiration

For my imagined product, I really wanted to create a coffee product and corresponding brand. I really enjoy how blue and yellow look together, and I thought that I could create a cool brand with these colors that is based on night time and celestial imagery. I came up with the catch phrase "Wake up. Don't sleep. The best things happen after dark" and I ran with it. I wanted the brand to be firmly based on the idea that so many awesome things can happen at night, and that the consumer needs a product that can help them accomplish these things. Below, you can see my initial mood board for the brand and product design.

Branding

My next step was to start ironing out the details of the brand itself, and the design aspects. This is when I chose a specific color palette of colors that I believed to work together nicely and would create a good composition. Additionally, I designed the icon, picked the font, and named the company "Full Moon Roasts". Below you can see the icon that I hand drew, as well as the color palette and all the content that I would use for the packaging.
