week nine: multi shaft album weave

This assignment was completed on October 22, 2023

My goal for this weekly assignment was to experiment with the baby wolf floor loom in the ATLAS equipment room. I wanted to try to weave from a draft for the first time, and also understand how the loom works, and troubleshooting as problems came up.

draft

The draft that I used for the baby wolf was the draft that was posted as an example. I did this because last minute I had to change my loom plans to baby wolf, and I didn't have enough time to remake a draft. I happened to (luckily) love the draft pattern, and as a complete beginner I chose to go that route and I am really happy with it.

reflection

As a whole, I loved working on the floor loom. I thought that it was so much fun, and really therapeutic. I also was really proud of how my final swatch came out. Because of this assignment, I now am confident in troubleshooting the loom when there are uneven tensions, as well as a ripped warp. For the final, I would love to work on the floor loom again.
